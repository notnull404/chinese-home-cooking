Learn how to make Xiangjian鲈yukuai (Pan-fried sea bass nuggets) with this easy recipe.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| 鲈鱼(加州鲈) | 1条(650克) |
| 小青椒 | 2-3个 |
| 小米辣 | 1-2个 |

## Seasonings

- 盐 3~5克
- 生抽 10~15克
- 白糖 5克
- 白胡椒粉 约1克
- 玉米淀粉 10-15克
- 食用油 15克左右
- 姜丝 3-5片切丝
- 香葱段 2~3根切段

## Instructions

1. Prepare ingredients.\n2. Cook according to recipe.\n3. Serve and enjoy.

## Tips

For best results, use fresh ingredients.
