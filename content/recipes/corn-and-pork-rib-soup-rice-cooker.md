Sweet corn and tender pork ribs simmered into a golden, nourishing broth. Made effortlessly in a rice cooker.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| pork ribs | 400g |
| corn | 2 pcs |
| carrot | 1 pc |
| red dates | 5 pcs |
| ginger | 3 slices |

## Seasonings

- ginger slices
- salt
- cooking wine
- green onion
- MSG (optional)

## Instructions

1. Wash all ingredients. Cut corn and carrot into chunks. Slice ginger. Cut green onion into sections.

2. Blanch ribs in cold water with 1 tbsp cooking wine to remove impurities. Wash clean.

3. Place ribs, carrot, corn, green onion, and ginger slices into the rice cooker pot.

4. Add water and season with salt and MSG (optional). Set the rice cooker to 'Slow Soup' mode and cook until done.


## Tips

Adjust salt level after the soup is done, not before. The water should just cover all ingredients. For a clearer broth, blanch the ribs first.
