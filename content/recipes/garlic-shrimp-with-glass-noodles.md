A stunning dinner party dish that's secretly easy — shrimp and noodles steamed with fragrant garlic butter.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| shrimp | 300g |
| glass noodles | 100g |
| garlic | 6 cloves |
| green onion | 2 stalks |

## Seasonings

- light soy sauce
- oyster sauce
- cooking wine
- salt
- sugar

## Instructions

1. Soak glass noodles in warm water until soft. Drain and spread on a plate.

2. Peel and devein the shrimp, leaving the tails on. Butterfly by cutting along the back and flattening gently.

3. Arrange shrimp on top of the noodles.

4. Mince the entire head of garlic into fine pieces.

5. Fry half the garlic in oil until light golden. Mix with the raw half to create 'gold & silver garlic'.

6. Add light soy sauce, oyster sauce, cooking wine, sugar, and salt to the garlic mixture.

7. Spread the garlic sauce over the shrimp. Steam over boiling water on high heat for exactly 5 minutes.

8. Garnish with green onion and bird's eye chili. Pour a splash of hot oil over the top to sizzle.


## Tips

The 'gold & silver garlic' (half fried, half raw) is the secret to this dish. Do not steam for more than 6 minutes. The final drizzle of hot oil over the scallions is essential.
