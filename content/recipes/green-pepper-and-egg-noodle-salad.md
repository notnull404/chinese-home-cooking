Chewy noodles tossed with scrambled eggs and shredded green bell peppers in a savory soy-vinegar dressing for a satisfying 15-minute meal.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| eggs | 2 piece |
| green bell peppers | 1 piece |

## Seasonings

- light soy sauce
- dark soy sauce
- oyster sauce
- cornstarch
- yellow soybean paste
- vinegar
- scallion, ginger, garlic

## Instructions

1. 先把酱汁调好，一定要放yellow soybean paste，这是灵魂。调好后可以加适量的water搅匀，也可以少放一点点salt。
2. 先煮noodles，捞出后放入cold water。再炒eggs，炒chili peppers，倒入料汁，沸腾后倒入eggs，料汁变粘稠状即可盛出。
3. 把green bell pepperseggs和noodles搅拌就做好啦！

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
