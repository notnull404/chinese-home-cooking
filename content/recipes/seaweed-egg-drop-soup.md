A 5-minute soup that's light, comforting, and full of umami from seaweed and shrimp.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| seaweed | 10g |
| egg | 2 pcs |
| green onion | 1 stalk |

## Seasonings

- salt
- sesame oil
- white pepper

## Instructions

1. Tear dried seaweed into small pieces and place in a large soup bowl. Add dried shrimp.

2. Beat the egg. Chop green onion. Julienne ginger.

3. Bring 2 bowls of water to a boil with ginger shreds.

4. Reduce to low heat until the surface is barely rippling.

5. Slowly drizzle the egg into the water in a thin stream while stirring gently with chopsticks — do not stir after pouring.

6. Immediately pour the hot soup into the bowl with seaweed. Add salt, sesame oil, white pepper, and green onion. Serve.


## Tips

Don't boil the seaweed — pour the hot soup over it directly. Let the water barely ripple when adding the egg for the most delicate ribbons. Dried shrimp add a huge umami boost.
