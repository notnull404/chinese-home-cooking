Tender eggplant stir-fried and braised with sweet bell peppers and tomatoes in a savory garlic sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| 甜椒 |  |
| tomatoes |  |
| eggs |  |

## Seasonings

- garlic
- light soy sauce

## Instructions

1. 放入cornstarch搅拌
2. 3勺cornstarch、3勺flour、eggs一个，搅成面糊，Chinese eggplant放入滚一圈
3. 起油烧锅炸至金黄，捞出控油
4. 料汁（garlic切碎、一勺light soy sauce、半勺dark soy sauce、一勺oyster sauce、半勺sugar、适量water，少许chicken bouillon）搅拌均匀
5. 起锅烧油：倒入tomatoes、甜椒，倒入料汁，加入Chinese eggplant，翻炒均匀即可

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
