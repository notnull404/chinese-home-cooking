Crispy, layered scallion pancakes made with a yeast-risen dough for a soft interior and golden exterior.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| eggs | 3 piece |
| yeast |  |
| warm water | 270 |

## Seasonings

- salt
- flour
- 油
- scallion

## Instructions

1. 蛋液中+warm water+salt+yeast，静置5分钟，+flour+油，搅拌止无干粉颗粒，发酵至2倍大
2. 切scallions花，拌到发酵好的面团中
3. 分剂子15个，醒发15分钟
4. 用手轻轻压平，扯开
5. 少许油润锅，中火！！！，放入饼坯，约2分钟起泡翻面钟，再1分半钟起锅。。。OK啦，每天冰箱中拿出放锅内回下温或微波叮30秒，一样好吃，不硬不干。

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
