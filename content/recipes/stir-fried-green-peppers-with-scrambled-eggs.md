A timeless Chinese comfort dish where tender green peppers meet fluffy scrambled eggs in a light soy glaze.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| green bell peppers | 8 piece |
| eggs | 4 piece |

## Seasonings

- garlic
- salt
- sesame oil
- cooking wine
- light soy sauce

## Instructions

1. chili peppers一分为二，斜刀切 garlic切片 eggs打散加入少许salt跟sesame seedssesame oil、少许cooking wine搅匀
2. 起锅烧油，倒入eggs液，用筷子快速搅碎 不要太熟，成型就可以出锅，装盘里待用
3. 热锅放少许油，放入garlic跟青chili peppers翻炒，放少许salt翻炒均匀。待chili peppers炒至断生，倒入eggs翻炒均匀倒入light soy sauce调味即可

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
