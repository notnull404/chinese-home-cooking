An incredibly easy soup made in the rice cooker with pork ribs, Chinese yam, and chestnuts for a naturally sweet and savory broth.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| pork ribs | 600g |
| Chinese yam | 1 stalk |
| 板栗 | 400g |

## Seasonings

- ginger
- cooking wine
- salt
- scallion

## Instructions

1. pork ribs提前泡出血water后洗净，锅中放water，pork ribs冷water下锅，放cooking wine，几片生ginger，焯water捞出。
2. pork ribs放入电饭煲，加ginger片和板栗。
3. 加适量water没过食材。
4. 选择炖汤模式，炖1个小时。 *没有炖汤模式可以直接用煮饭键，时间保证在一个小时左右就可以
5. 趁这个时候把Chinese yam去皮切小段。 *记得一定要戴手套，避免接触Chinese yam手发痒
6. 剩余15分钟的时候，放入Chinese yam、适量salt，继续炖煮。
7. 最后装盘撒点scallions花就可以啦~
8. 放进电饭煲一键搞定，秋天一定要多喝~

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
