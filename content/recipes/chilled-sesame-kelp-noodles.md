Tender kelp strips tossed in a tangy sesame-garlic dressing for a light, refreshing cold appetizer.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| kelp | 150g |

## Seasonings

- vinegar
- sugar
- salt
- garlic
- soy sauce
- MSG

## Instructions

1. kelp丝炒熟过cold water控干，所有调料放里面
2. 抓烂均匀，我喜欢提前半小时拌好入味

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
