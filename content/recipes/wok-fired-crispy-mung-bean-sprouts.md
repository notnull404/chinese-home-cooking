A lightning-fast stir-fry that keeps mung bean sprouts crunchy and juicy without turning soggy, finished with a touch of scallion and vinegar.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| mung bean sprouts | 500g |

## Seasonings

- garlic
- dried chili peppers
- Sichuan peppercorns
- cilantro
- light soy sauce
- aromatic vinegar

## Instructions

1. mung bean sproutswater洗净，充分沥干water分备用。所有配料切好备用
2. 锅中倒油烧热，下入scallionsgarlic、Sichuan peppercorns和dried chili peppers小火爆香。
3. 转最大火，倒入沥干的mung bean sprouts，快速颠锅翻炒 1 分钟。
4. 加入light soy sauce、salt、少许sugar，沿锅边淋入aromatic vinegar。保持大火继续翻炒至bean sprouts微微断生，撒cilantro即可关火装盘。

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
