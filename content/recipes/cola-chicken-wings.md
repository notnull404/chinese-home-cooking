A foolproof beginner dish — chicken wings braised in cola until sticky, sweet, and fall-off-the-bone tender.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| chicken wings | 8 pcs |
| cola | 1 can (330ml) |
| ginger | 5 slices |
| green onion | 2 stalks |

## Seasonings

- light soy sauce
- dark soy sauce
- cooking wine
- salt

## Instructions

1. Score two slits on each side of the chicken wings.

2. Blanch wings in cold water with ginger and cooking wine. Drain.

3. Heat a little oil. Fry wings skin-side down until golden on both sides.

4. Pour in cola until it just covers the wings. Add 2 tbsp light soy sauce, 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce, and ginger slices.

5. Bring to a boil, then reduce to medium-low heat. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.

6. Uncover and turn up the heat to reduce the sauce until thick and sticky. Garnish with green onion.


## Tips

Pan-frying the wings first removes excess fat and adds flavor. Cola has enough sugar — no extra sugar needed.
