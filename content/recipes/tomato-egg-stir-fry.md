China's #1 home-style dish. Sweet tomatoes + silky eggs = pure comfort in under 10 minutes.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| tomato | 3 pcs |
| egg | 4 pcs |
| green onion | 2 stalks |
| garlic | 2 cloves |

## Seasonings

- salt
- sugar
- ketchup
- cooking oil

## Instructions

1. Score an X on the bottom of each tomato, blanch in boiling water, then peel and cut into wedges.

2. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt and a splash of water until frothy.

3. Heat oil in a wok until smoking. Pour in eggs, stir quickly with chopsticks until just set, then remove.

4. Leave a little oil. Sauté garlic until fragrant. Add tomatoes and stir-fry over medium heat until saucy.

5. Add 1 tbsp ketchup and 1 tsp sugar to enhance flavor.

6. Return eggs to the wok, tossing to coat in the tomato sauce. Season with salt. Garnish with green onion.


## Tips

Add a splash of water to the beaten eggs for extra fluffiness. Use ripe, soft tomatoes for the best sauce. Cook on high heat throughout for the best texture.
