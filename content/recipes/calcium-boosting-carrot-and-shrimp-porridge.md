A nourishing toddler-friendly congee with carrots, shiitake mushrooms, dried shrimp, and greens.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| carrot | 半 stalk |
| shiitake mushrooms | 2-3 piece |
| dried shrimp | 两 tbsp |
| 青菜叶 | 1-2 slices |


## Instructions

1. carrot，shiitake mushrooms，dried shrimp切碎备用
2. 锅里倒一点点油，倒入dried shrimp炒香
3. 倒入carrotshiitake mushrooms翻炒均匀
4. 倒入煮好的白粥煮
5. 放一点salt调味
6. 加入青菜碎再煮一分钟
7. 美味好吃的粥就做好了
