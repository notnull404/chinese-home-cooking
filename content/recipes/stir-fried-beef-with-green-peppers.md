Thinly sliced beef wok-seared with green bell peppers in a black bean garlic sauce for a classic Chinese comfort dish.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| beef | 300克 |
| green bell peppers | 6 piece |

## Seasonings

- ginger
- garlic
- cornstarch
- cooking wine
- light soy sauce

## Instructions

1. gingergarlic切末，green bell peppers切丝，beef逆纹理切丝。beef放入一勺light soy sauce，半勺cooking wine，一勺cornstarch，一勺油抓匀腌制10分钟。
2. 锅内倒少许油，油热后倒入beef丝，炒至变色后盛出。
3. 锅内倒少许油，爆香gingergarlic，加入green bell peppers丝翻炒，倒一勺light soy sauce和少量salt翻炒，把刚炒好的beef丝倒在一起翻炒均匀即可出锅。
