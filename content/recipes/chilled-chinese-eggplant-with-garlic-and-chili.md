Steamed eggplant shredded and served cold with a fragrant garlic, soy, and millet chili dressing.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Chinese eggplant | 2 piece |
| millet |  |

## Seasonings

- garlic
- scallion
- light soy sauce
- oyster sauce
- sugar
- cilantro

## Instructions

1. Chinese eggplant洗净后切成段，放入蒸锅中，water开后蒸8分钟；
2. 蒸熟后放凉一会儿，用手撕成条状；
3. garlic末、millet椒、scallions花按量切好；
4. 淋热油爆香，油不用多；
5. 再放light soy sauce4勺，oyster sauce2勺，sugar1勺；
6. 把酱汁浇在Chinese eggplant上；
7. 喜欢吃cilantro的再撒一把cilantro，全部搅拌均匀即可；
8. 赶紧做起来吧～

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
