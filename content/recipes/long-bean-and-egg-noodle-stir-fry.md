Chewy noodles stir-fried with fresh long beans and scrambled eggs in a savory soy-based sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| 豇豆 |  |
| eggs |  |

## Seasonings

- flour
- salt

## Instructions

1. flour加2克salt，用cold water和面，软硬适中
2. 和好用保鲜膜盖好醒面30分钟
3. 豇豆洗净控干water分
4. 切成约5cm的段
5. 面醒好，切成比包饺子用的面剂小一些
6. 拿一根green beans裹上面剂
7. 在案板上搓一下，裹在green beans上
8. water开下green beans面，green beans煮熟，面就熟了
9. 醒面时，炒个tomatoeseggs卤，菜卤熟时加放入出了河南就吃不到的荆芥
10. 再凉拌个cucumber丝，拌面
11. 菜饭合一，美味健康

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
