Golden pan-fried tofu braised in a rich scallion and soy sauce glaze until each piece is savory and tender.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| firm tofu | 1 piece |

## Seasonings

- scallions

## Instructions

1. scallions切段加两勺light soy sauce、一勺dark soy sauce、salt、胡椒少许一勺cornstarch、一勺oyster sauce适量water搅拌均匀
2. tofu掰成小块 油热下锅煎至金黄
3. 倒入scallions料汁
4. 翻炒至汤汁浓稠

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
