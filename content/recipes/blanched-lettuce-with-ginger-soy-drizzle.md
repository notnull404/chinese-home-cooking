Crisp lettuce quickly blanched and drizzled with a warm ginger-soy oil for the purest vegetable flavor.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| lettuce | 500克 |

## Seasonings

- 油
- garlic
- salt
- light soy sauce
- oyster sauce

## Instructions

1. 准备lettuce清洗干净，待用。
2. 锅倒water煮开，加入salt和油，倒入lettucewater开煮至断生发软，盛出装盘。
3. 热锅冷油放进garlic末小火爆香，倒入light soy sauce+oyster sauce关火，盛出淋到烫好待用的菜上面。

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
