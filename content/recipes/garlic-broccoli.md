Bright green, crunchy broccoli with fragrant garlic. The healthiest dish that actually tastes amazing.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| broccoli | 300g |
| garlic | 5 cloves |

## Seasonings

- salt
- oyster sauce
- cooking oil

## Instructions

1. Cut broccoli into small florets. Soak in salted water for 10 minutes, then rinse.

2. Bring water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and a drop of oil. Blanch broccoli for exactly 60 seconds.

3. Transfer immediately to ice water to stop cooking. Drain well.

4. Finely mince garlic.

5. Slowly fry garlic in cold oil over low heat until light golden and fragrant.

6. Turn heat to high. Add broccoli, oyster sauce, and salt. Stir-fry quickly and serve.


## Tips

Adding oil and salt to the blanching water keeps the broccoli bright green. Fry garlic slowly over low heat to avoid bitterness. Do not overcook — 60 seconds max.
