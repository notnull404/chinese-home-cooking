A surprisingly delicious diet-friendly pancake combining shredded eggplant with eggs for a guilt-free meal.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Chinese eggplant | 1 stalk |
| eggs | 1 piece |

## Seasonings

- scallion
- flour

## Instructions

1. Chinese eggplant切片少许salt腌出water分，拧干
2. Chinese eggplant里加入eggs，flour，少许黑胡椒，scallions花；搅拌均匀
3. 少油放入饼，最小火🔥煎3-5分钟，煎至两面金黄即可

## Tips

For the best results, prepare all ingredients before you start cooking (mise en place).
