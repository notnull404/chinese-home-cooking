Learn how to make 最简单的bai灼xia（shuizhu）yanshui基尾xia (The simplest white cauterized shrimp (boiled) brine-based tail shrimp) with this easy recipe.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| 基围虾 | 1斤（大约20多只） |

## Seasonings

- 姜片 4-6片
- 料酒 2大勺
- 盐 两小勺
- 大葱 4段
- 或者小葱 4-5根
- 生抽 1勺
- 醋 4滴

## Instructions

1. Prepare ingredients.\n2. Cook according to recipe.\n3. Serve and enjoy.

## Tips
