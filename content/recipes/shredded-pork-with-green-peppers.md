A simple, everyday stir-fry that never gets old. Tender pork and crisp peppers in a savory sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| pork tenderloin | 250g |
| green pepper | 3 pcs |
| garlic | 3 cloves |
| ginger | 3 slices |

## Seasonings

- light soy sauce
- dark soy sauce
- cooking wine
- cornstarch
- salt
- sugar

## Instructions

1. Slice pork into thin shreds. Marinate with light soy sauce, cooking wine, and cornstarch for 10 minutes.

2. Seed and julienne the green peppers. Slice garlic. Julienne ginger.

3. Heat oil in a wok. Stir-fry pork shreds until just cooked. Remove.

4. Leave some oil. Sauté ginger and garlic until fragrant. Add green peppers and stir-fry.

5. Return pork to the wok. Season with light soy sauce, salt, and a pinch of sugar. Toss well and serve.


## Tips

Slice the pork along the grain for tender shreds. Use high heat and quick cooking to keep the peppers crisp.
