Extra-flavorful braised eggplant with tomatoes and green peppers in a rich sauce that rivals any restaurant.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| tomatoes |  |
| green bell peppers |  |
| eggs |  |

## Seasonings

- garlic
- light soy sauce
- sugar
- cornstarch
- 耗油
- salt
- flour

## Instructions

1. Chinese eggplant切块，舀进两勺干cornstarch，用手拌匀。tomatoes、green bell peppers切块，garlic切片
2. 调料汁：1勺cornstarch＋3勺water＋2勺soy sauce＋1勺耗油＋1勺sugar＋半勺salt
3. 3小勺flour，3小勺cornstarch，一个eggs，适量water调成面糊。Chinese eggplant放进去滚一圈，每面都要粘上面糊
4. 滚好后下油锅炸，炸到这样金黄色捞出。
5. 锅留底油，下garlic瓣爆香后加tomatoes、green bell peppers大火炒
6. 炒至断生后加入调好的料汁，煮开以后放Chinese eggplant
7. 翻炒均匀就可以出锅了！

## Tips

料汁的比例是我们家喜欢的口味，大家调出来后可以先尝一下，根据自己喜好加salt或sugar
